Cheeseburger

Description

This classic dish consists of a beef patty topped with cheese between bread buns, and various other fillings that are up to the consumer.

Steps

  1. Cook the beef patty
  2. Add the cheese slice
  3. Toast the buns
  4. Brush buns with mayo, ketchup, mustard, or whatever burger sauce you choose
  5. Add toppings (lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, etc)
  6. Eat
